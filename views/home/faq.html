How do I login?

You can SSH into your machines with your federal credentials eg. ssh fed12345@vm20.nubes.stfc.ac.uk. You can also associate your SSH public key to your account for passwordless login.


Can I get root access?

Yes, once you have logged in with your federal credentials you will have permission to use sudo.

NOTE: you must not change the root password or remove entires from the authorized_keys file for the root account. Please make sure you have read the Terms of Service.


Can I get a GUI session?

Click the button to get an interactive GUI session inside your browser.

What are projects?

Projects are groups that work together, every member of a project will be able to see all VMs in said project. Use the drop-down in the top right of the page to switch between projects.


Can I create/join another project?

Yes, if you would like to create/join another project email us at {{ email }}.


Can I rename my VM?

Yes, simply mouseover the VM name and click the button that appears. It may take a few seconds for the name to update.


What are flavors?

Flavors define the resources assigned to a VM when it is created. Once you have chosen a template, you will be prompted to choose a flavor from the drop-down.

What is the STOPPING state?

When a VM is deleted, a snapshop is taken an stored for a period of time. This is for the reason of security and traceability.


Do you support other Linux variants/version?

Why are Pakiti and rsyslog running on my VM?

These services are used for monitoring and security. Please refrain from stopping or changing the behaviour of either of these service. Please make sure you have read the Terms of Service.
